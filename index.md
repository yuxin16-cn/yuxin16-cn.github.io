# Welcome to Intent & Scope.

# Technically right. Market wrong.
Exploring where technical correctness diverges from regulatory reality — across AI, cybersecurity, and compliance systems.

## What this site is about

This is not a blog about standards summaries or compliance checklists.

It is a collection of observations on how technical validation, regulatory frameworks, and market realities interact — and often misalign.

Most writing here focuses on:
- AI and cybersecurity compliance
- Regulatory interpretation vs implementation
- Evidence, audit logic, and responsibility boundaries

## How to read this site

The content is not organized by topic, but by how problems are analyzed:

- **01｜Language Drift** — where terminology starts to distort meaning  
- **02｜Hidden Assumptions** — what must be true for a judgment to hold  
- **03｜Audit Logic** — how evidence is constructed and evaluated  
- **04｜Regulatory Signals** — how rules evolve beyond written texts  
- **05｜Tools ≠ Judgment** — where technical tools stop being enough  
- **∞｜Notes / Observations** — edge cases and ongoing thinking

## Why this matters

Compliance failures rarely come from missing requirements.
They come from using correct answers in the wrong context.
